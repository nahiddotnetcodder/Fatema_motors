About Us

AK Trade International is proud to offer you a variety of disposable surgical instruments to best fit surgeon and patient needs.   In additional to reusable surgical equipment, our single-use options expiate the device preparation process.

AK Trade International has over 10 years of experience in the Surgical product, where they have been committed to producing innovative and effective products at affordable prices that meet the needs of both our customers and their patients. Surgeons may choose from an array of disposable surgical instruments.  We offer prepackaged, sterile dilators, k-wire, light cables and surgical screws.  Disposable surgical instruments from AK Trade International Surgical Innovations maintains product integrity while ensuring sterility. 

Choosing to use disposable instruments reduces the risk of cross infection.   Potential microbiological contamination is easily avoided by using any of disposable options.   The sterility of undamaged and unopened disposable products is guaranteed until the expiration date.  Our single-use instruments function identically to reusable equipment, and are made from only the highest quality material.

Quality Policy
