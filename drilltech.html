About Us

The DrillTech International is a leading provider of Horizontal Directional Drilling throughout Bangladesh. Horizontal directional drilling or HDD is a trenchless method of installing underground pipes, conduits and cables in a shallow arc along a prescribed bore path by using a surface-launched or pit-launched drilling rig. Directional boring is used when trenching or excavating is not practical. With HDD, it is possible to drill under roadways, river crossings, and mountainous regions with less equipment at a lower cost, with a minimum period of time and minimal impact on the surrounding area.

DrillTech International has a team of experienced managers, engineers’ backgrounds in the Computer Engineering civil engineering and construction industry. We are also a civil and construction company that has built a strong reputation for delivering a quality service, on time, and at a competitive price.
