About Us

We are pleased to introduce our company, Fatema motors we are a trading company authorized auto spare parts in Dhaka -Bangladesh. We are the leading supplier of all sources of auto parts for Korean & Japan’s cars.

The supply of parts for Korean cars has been limited throughout the world. We have greater purchasing power! We can offer best our customers with more competitive price and most efficient and satisfactory services we have built up stable and long-term corporation with costumers all around the Country.  And the associated management have created a highly experienced sales team that expand of auto part products countrywide. 


We supply all kinds of auto spare parts for Korean cars such as Hyundai, Kia. We are able to send parts direct from Korea to anywhere in Bangladesh. We have a wide range of Original/ OEM/ Non- genuine / Rebuilt auto spare parts with top quality from selected manufacturers.
